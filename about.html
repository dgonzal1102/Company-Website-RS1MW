About Us

With a lifetime of automotive passion, RS1 Motorworks, in partnership with Vault Auto Services, is committed to offering an exclusive line of high-quality products and services in an inviting and professional atmosphere.

RS1 Motorworks is located in San Antonio, TX and is your premier dealer of select luxury and exotic motorcars. We specialize in providing our customers with an incredible concierge sales experience that begins with offering each and every qualified Client a look into one of the most meticulous collection of preserved, award-winning motorcars available for purchase. If you don’t find what you are looking for in our current inventory, our devoted sales staff will aggressively search for the car of your dreams through our esteemed network of automobile enthusiasts, memberships and societies, ensuring you get exactly what you are looking for.

Our services include private owner consignments, various levels of auto detailing and restoration, long and short term, temperature- and humidity-controlled storage, standard vehicle maintenance and more.
