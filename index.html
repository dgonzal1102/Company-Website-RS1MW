RS1 Motorworks offers the discerning buyer a private galley of ultra high-end motorcars by appointment only. Whether a Uber-luxury automobile or a Super Car, RS1 Motorworks can arrange a direct sale of one of our restored vehicles, or brokerage and customization on assignment, locating and restoring a selected motorcar to Touring Class, Premium Touring Class, or Concours level.

The commissioning of RS1 Motorworks by year/make and model allows our specialists to locate the vehicle desired, purchase the motorcar, and restore the motorcar to one of the three levels noted above--making the assignment a customized commissioning and allowing our clients to select colors and accessories--in fact allowing our clients to bespoke the motorcar as if it were being built for the first time!

Whether you are looking for a proper and correct example, or a personalized or custom-built motorcar, our specialists are available for private consultation. A phone interview is normally the first step with an appointment thereafter to visit our workshop and view projects underway or already completed.

Contact us and let us know what type, make or model you are interested in to begin your commissioned private search and restoration project.
